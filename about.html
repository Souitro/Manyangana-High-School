About Manyangana High School

School of choice comitted to excellence...

Manyangana High School is a public secondary school located at Stand No 20001, Uthatrusthluvukani, Bushbuckridge Rural, 1363 in Mpumalanga Province, South Africa. The Schoolâ€™s post office box number is at Private Bag X 1404 Bushbuckridge Hluvukani 1363. Manyangana High School is an ordinary school specializing in ordinary subjects.

The school is a Section 21 school meaning it is allocated finances by the department and is responsible for ordering stationery, textbooks, paying water and lights accounts and undertaking their own maintenance. it is classified under quintile 1.

According to a past survey conducted on the year 2016, the school had a total population of 604 learners served by 21 teachers, making a student-teacher ratio of 29:1. The school EMIS number is 800035031 and mail box address at Private Bag X1404, Hluvukani, 1363.

Matric Performance

The 2020 matric class of Manyangana High School obtained a 87.6% pass rate lower than 97.2% attained in the year 2019 National Senior Certificate Examination. The 137 learners wrote the exam in 2020 from 108 in 2019. From 2018 to 2020, the school attained an average of 94.9% pass rate.
